The guidelines provide a standardized, risk-based notification process in the instance of a security breach. U.S. Department of Homeland Security image The IPSC also reviews the Personal Information (PI) System Annual Reports submitted by state and county government agencies, pursuant to HRS §487N-7 , and submits an annual summary report to the Legislature with the IPSC’s findings, significant trends, and recommendations to protect personal information used by government agencies. State and county agencies’ PI System Annual Reports are due to the IPSC by Sept. 30 each year.
